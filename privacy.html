Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

1. Summary

EcoLearn stores minimal data client-side to enhance your learning experience, such as your XP, level, chosen title, and profile picture. We do not collect or store personal data on servers.

2. Data We Store

  • XP and level progress (for leaderboards and achievements)
  • Display name, profile title, and bio
  • Profile picture (stored as a data URL in your browser)
  • Basic preferences (like selected titles)

All data above is stored in your browser's localStorage and never transmitted to a server by EcoLearn.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party CDNs (Tailwind, AOS, Feather Icons) to deliver assets. These providers may receive standard web request data (e.g., IP address) when your browser fetches those assets.

4. Cookies

EcoLearn does not set cookies. Progress is stored in localStorage on your device.

5. Data Control

You can reset or remove your data by clearing your browser's site data for EcoLearn. This action will reset XP, level, and profile settings.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please visit our Contact page.
