Health Guidelines

Evidence-informed guidance for everyday health and hygiene.

Respiratory Etiquette

Cover coughs/sneezes with a tissue or your elbow; dispose of tissues immediately. Wash hands after coughing/sneezing.

Food Safety

  • Keep raw and cooked foods separate.
  • Cook meats to safe internal temperatures.
  • Refrigerate perishables within 2 hours.

Water & Sanitation

Drink safe water; treat or boil if unsure. Maintain clean bathrooms and ensure proper waste disposal.

Sick-Day Guidance

If unwell, rest, hydrate, and minimize close contact. Seek professional medical advice for persistent or severe symptoms.
