Hygiene Tips

Practical, science-backed tips you can use daily.

Hand Hygiene

  • Wash for at least 20 seconds with soap and water.
  • Use sanitizer (≥60% alcohol) when soap isn’t available.
  • Clean under nails and between fingers.

Oral Care

  • Brush twice daily and floss once.
  • Replace toothbrush every 3–4 months.
  • Visit a dentist every 6 months.

Personal Hygiene

  • Shower daily or every other day based on activity.
  • Wear clean clothes; let shoes air dry.
  • Keep nails trimmed and clean.

Device & Home

  • Disinfect phone and keyboard daily.
  • Change bed sheets every 1–2 weeks.
  • Sanitize kitchen sponges daily; replace weekly.
